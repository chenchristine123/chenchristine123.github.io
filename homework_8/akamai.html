Overview:
For 4 months over Summer 2020, I worked as an design intern on the Creative team in Akamai technologies. I led designs for the company's online brand guidelines aiming to improve accessibility across 57 locations and 29 countries. I conceptualized and created information architecture, wireframes, user flow, and interactive prototypes. I also developed visuals for social media, client newsletters, and virtual events. In addition, I contributed to company wide goals to increase cohesion through designing video conference backgrounds for remote work settings.
